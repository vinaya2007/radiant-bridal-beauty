

# Sri Sai Sakthi Beauty Parlor & Mehandi — Website Plan

## Design Theme
- **Colors**: Rose pink (#E8A0BF), soft gold (#D4A574), cream white (#FFF8F0), light beige (#F5E6D3)
- **Style**: Elegant, feminine, traditional Indian beauty inspired with mehendi patterns, floral accents
- **Mobile-first** responsive design

## Pages & Structure

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero**: Full-width bridal banner with headline, CTA buttons (Book Appointment, View Mehendi), Google rating badge (⭐ 4.9)
- **Trust Bar**: Icons for Google Rating, Bridal Specialist, Friendly Parlour, Serving Thanjavur
- **About Preview**: Short intro paragraph with photo
- **Featured Services**: 6 cards (Bridal Makeup, Mehendi, Ear Piercing, Facials, Threading, Waxing)
- **Gallery Preview**: 6-image grid with "View Full Gallery" button
- **Testimonials Carousel**: Customer review cards with star ratings
- **CTA Banner**: "Book Your Bridal Makeup or Mehendi Appointment Today"

### 2. About Page
- Story of the parlour, values, bridal expertise, hygiene focus
- Workspace/parlour photo placeholder

### 3. Services Page
- **Bridal Makeup**: Traditional, Reception, Engagement, Party makeup with benefits list
- **Mehendi Services**: Bridal, Festival, Custom designs with gallery preview
- **Ear Piercing**: Safety and hygiene details
- **Beauty Treatments**: Threading, Facials, Waxing, Grooming

### 4. Mehendi Gallery Page
- Modern masonry/grid gallery with hover animations
- Categories: Bridal, Festival, Full hand, Close-up art

### 5. Bridal Portfolio Page
- Bridal makeup showcase, before-after transformations
- "Custom bridal packages available" note

### 6. Testimonials Page
- 5-6 customer reviews in elegant card layout with star ratings

### 7. Book Appointment Page
- Form: Name, Phone, Service, Date, Time, Message
- WhatsApp booking button (links to 6379262005)

### 8. Contact Page
- Business info, address, phone, working hours
- Google Maps embed
- WhatsApp contact button

## Global Components
- **Navbar**: Responsive with mobile hamburger menu, all 8 navigation items
- **Footer**: Quick links, services, contact info, Google rating, WhatsApp button
- **Floating WhatsApp Button**: Fixed bottom-right on all pages
- **SEO**: Meta titles/descriptions with local keywords

## UX Features
- Smooth scrolling, hover effects, image animations, soft transitions
- Placeholder images using elegant gradient/pattern placeholders
- Fast loading, mobile-optimized layout
